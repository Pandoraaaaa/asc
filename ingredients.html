Ingredients

Agricultural Software Consultants, Inc. (ASC) provides ingredient and nutrient data from a variety of sources, including National Research Council (NRC) publications. Users of MixitWin can enter data from any source or change the data that ASC provides. ASC data are not required and are only provided as a way of avoiding data entry.

USDA

  • The USDA National Database for Standard Reference, Release 28 (2015), with 8790 ingredients and 150 nutrients, including 57 fatty acids and 5 sterols. This data is also available at http://www.ars.usda.gov/ba/bhnrc/ndl
  • USDA human foods for pets. This database has 150 nutrients and 141 ingredients selected from the USDA National Nutrient Database (2015) with example dog and cat diets. Compiled by Dr. Rebecca L Remillard, Ph.D, D.V.M., DACVN, and Dr. Sarah K Abood, D.V.M., Ph.D of Veterinary Nutritional Consultations, Inc.

Beef Cattle

  • Beef 1996 NRC - 86 ingredients, 26 nutrients from Table 11.1 of the NRC publication "Nutrient Requirements of Beef Cattle," seventh revised edition, 1996.
  • Beef 1984 NRC - 258 ingredients, 32 nutrients from the NRC "Nutrient Requirements of Beef Cattle," sixth revised edition, 1984.
  • Spanish Beef 1984 NRC - same as above with ingredient and nutrient names in Spanish.

Dairy Cattle

  • Dairy 2001 NRC - 271 ingredients, various nutrients from NRC 2001.
  • Dairy 1989 NRC (American/Metric/Spanish) - Variants of the NRC "Nutrient Requirements of Dairy Cattle," sixth revised edition, Update 1989.
  • Dairy 1978 NRC - From NRC 1978 fifth revised edition.

Swine

  • Swine 1998 NRC - Tables 11-1 to 11-8 from NRC swine 1998.
  • Swine 1988 NRC - Data from NRC tables and publication by Dr. Palmer Holden.

Poultry

  • Poultry 1994 NRC - 72 ingredients, 20 minerals, 44 nutrients, and 9 rations.
  • Spanish Poultry 1994 NRC - Same as above, in Spanish.

Broiler

  • Broiler data - 41 ingredients, 29 nutrients, 18 rations from Dr. Simon M. Shane, LSU.
  • Spanish Broiler - Same data in Spanish.

Gamebird

  • Compiled by Dr. Peter R. Ferket of NC State University – 92 ingredients, 57 nutrients for goose, duck, pheasant, quail, coturnix, and guinea fowl.

Turkey

  • Turkey data – 100+ ingredients, 57 nutrients, 40 diets for various scenarios.

USDA Database (again)

  • 150 nutrients, 8790 ingredients, standard serving sizes – USDA 2015.

Goat

  • Goat 1981 NRC – 271 ingredients, 27 nutrients from Tables 2–4.

Dog & Cat

  • Dogs and Cats 2006 NRC – Tables 13-1 to 13-8.
  • Dog and cat data compiled by Ray Sewell, Ph.D. – 132 ingredients, 54 nutrients.
  • Cat extrapolated data from NRC 1986.

Sheep

  • Sheep 1975 NRC – fifth revised edition, includes diets for ewes and lambs.

Ruminant

  • Small Ruminants 2007 NRC – Tables 15-11 to 15-13.

Horse

  • Horse 2007 NRC – Tables 16-6 and 16-7.
  • Horse 1989 NRC (Metric/American) – Table 6-1A.

Zoo

  • Zoo data – 303 ingredients, 41 nutrients, 124 rations compiled by R. S. Patton, Ph.D.

Mink & Fox

  • 137 ingredients with 6 mink and 2 fox diets.

Trout & Salmon

  • 67 ingredients, 45 nutrients, NRC tables 12–13. Compiled by Dr. Gary Rumsey and Dr. Steven Hughes.

Catfish

  • Data from Drs. Meng H. Li and Edwin H. Robinson, NRC 1993 + field research.

Tilapia

  • Compiled by same authors as catfish, NRC "Nutrient Requirements of Fish", 1993.

Shrimp

  • Two diets each for P. vannamei and P. monodon.

Finfish

  • Finfish 1993 NRC – Tables 8-1 through 8-4.

Blending Examples

  • Fertilizer – 28 ingredients, 17 minerals
  • Ice cream – Fat 16%, solids/sugar/egg/water
  • Sausage – Frankfurter blend: 70% beef/pork, 30% fat, 61% moisture

WARNING: These data files may not produce the desired results. A qualified nutritionist should check each ration before production. ASC does not guarantee the accuracy of the data. You should compare this data with values in appropriate publications.
